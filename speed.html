My Internet Speeds

I am in the fortunate position that my dad has a true
fibre to the premise internet connection. Which currently
is a 330Mbps Download and 50Mbps Upload. Whilst this is
the exception rather than the rule, here in the UK it has
remained stable at the quoted supply speeds for the last 4 years.
This is more than sufficent speed to run this website currently,
and also allows me to use my Playstation 4, and allow my dad
his computer, and the Sky Q box. Eventually more of the UK
will have speeds in excess of 100Mbps, which will be a great
game changer for the people that recieve these speeds.
